📖 How to Play Whot

Objective

Be the first player to play all your cards.

On Your Turn

  • Play a card that matches the shape OR number of the top card on the pile.
  • If you can't play, draw a card from the deck. You may then play the drawn card if it matches.

Special Cards

  • WHOT (20): Wild card. Play on anything and call a shape for the next player to match.
  • Hold On (1): You get to play again immediately.
  • Pick Two (5): Your opponent must draw 2 cards.
  • Suspension (8): Your opponent loses their next turn, and you play again.
  • General Market (14): Your opponent must draw 1 card.

Tips

  • Highlighted cards (gold border) in your hand are playable.
  • Click a card to play it.
  • Click Draw Card if you have no playable cards.
