Whot Go gratis Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled in the Whot Go gratis (Whot card game).

1. Information We Collect

Whot Go gratis does not require registration, does not require login, and does not directly collect personal data. We do not request your name, email address, phone number, home address, or user credentials.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect certain technical data to serve and measure ads, such as: device identifiers, approximate IP address, app usage information, and diagnostic data.

This data is managed by Google according to its own policies. More information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How Information Is Used

As the developer of Whot Go gratis, we do not use users' personal data because we do not collect it directly. Data use by AdMob is limited to ad delivery, personalization, and measurement according to Google's policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share users' personal data on our side. The only third-party integration in the app is Google AdMob.

5. Children

Whot Go gratis is a general audience game. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can review it.

6. Security

We apply reasonable measures to keep the app secure. However, no system is 100% infallible.

7. Changes to This Policy
